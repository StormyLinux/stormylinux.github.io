# Stormy Linux Developer Edition

## Version: 1.0

The Developer Edition of Stormy Linux is focused on software engineering and productivity, providing developers with a work environment that is minimal, feature rich, productive and easy to use. This edition of Stormy Linux will help you take your productive workflow to the next level.

This version will be shipping with additional features that can be leveraged to increase speed, ease of use and will provide functionalities that will make your workflow feel natural and comfortable.

The configuration of Stormy Linux Developer Edition is currently under construction and will be available soon...
